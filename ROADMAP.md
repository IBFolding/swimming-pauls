## Risk Mitigation

### Technical Risks
- **LLM costs** → Efficient routing, local models
- **API reliability** → Multiple providers, fallbacks
- **Scalability** → Microservices, caching

### Market Risks
- **Competition** → First-mover advantage, community
- **Regulation** → Decentralized architecture
- **Token volatility** → Utility-first design

### AI Risks
- **Alignment** → Transparent, interpretable
- **Safety** → Human-in-the-loop
- **Bias** → Diverse Paul personas
