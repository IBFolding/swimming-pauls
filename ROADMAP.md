## Economic Model

### Philosophy
**No subscriptions. No freemium tiers. No paywalls.**

All revenue flows back into the ecosystem:

```
User Fees → Mac Mini Hardware → More Pauls → Better Predictions
                ↓
        Creator Infrastructure
                ↓
        Community Treasury
```

### Fee Structure
- **Base usage:** Free (run locally on your machine)
- **Cloud predictions:** Small fee per query (gas + compute)
- **Premium features:** One-time unlocks, not subscriptions
- **All fees:** 100% to hardware buybacks + infrastructure

### Treasury Allocation
| Use Case | Allocation |
|----------|-----------|
| Hardware (Mac Minis) | 70% |
| Creator Infrastructure | 20% |
| Community Reserve | 10% |

### Transparency
- On-chain treasury (public ledger)
- Hardware purchases verified
- Monthly reports on expansion
- Community votes on major purchases
