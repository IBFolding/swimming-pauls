📖 THE ORIGIN STORY

It started on Sunset Boulevard. 2 AM. A self-driving Tesla with no one in it.

Inside, someone was listening to Swimming Paul on repeat, texting OpenClaw about MiroFish, and realizing... this is too complicated for regular people.

The idea hit like a lightning bolt: What if we made it simple?

No cloud APIs. No complicated setup. Just download, run, and cast the pool. Let 100+ AI personas argue about your question until they reach consensus.

"Let the Pauls cook."

🐟 WHAT IS SWIMMING PAULS?

Swimming Pauls is a multi-agent prediction engine that runs entirely on your machine. No cloud. No API keys. No data leaving your computer.

You ask a question. The Pauls argue about it. They debate, deliberate, and eventually reach consensus. Each Paul has a unique perspective:

  • 👨‍🏫 Professor Paul - Academic analysis
  • 📈 Trader Paul - Market sentiment
  • 🤨 Skeptic Paul - Risk management
  • 🔮 Visionary Paul - Long-term view
  • 🐋 Whale Paul - Institutional thinking
  • 🎰 Degen Paul - YOLO energy
🎯 How It Works
  1. 1. Connect your local agent
  2. 2. Ask any question
  3. 3. Pauls simulate & debate
  4. 4. Get consensus + confidence
🔒 Privacy First
Everything runs locally. Your questions never hit a server. Your data stays on your machine.
🤖 Local LLM Support
Connect to your own LLMs: Ollama, LM Studio, or any OpenAI-compatible API running locally.

🔌 CONNECT YOUR LOCAL LLM

The whole point: Your AI, your machine, your data. Connect to local LLMs for 100% private predictions.

🦙 Ollama
Run Llama 3, Mistral, or any model locally
ollama run llama3
🏠 LM Studio
GUI for local LLMs with OpenAI-compatible API
localhost:1234/v1
⚡ LocalAI
Self-hosted OpenAI API alternative
local-ai --models-path ./models
Why local? Your prompts never leave your machine. No API costs. No rate limits. Complete privacy. The Pauls use your local models to generate responses—each Paul can even use a different model for diverse perspectives.
